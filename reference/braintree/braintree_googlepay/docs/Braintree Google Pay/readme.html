Braintree Google Pay

by Numinix


Installation
  1. Install Braintree Payments for Zen Cart.
  2. Install Numinix Premium Plugin Installer version 2.0.0 or newer.
  3. Backup your database and all files.
  4. Upload all files in this package while maintaining the directory structure (rename YOUR_ADMIN to your admin folder).
  5. Log into your Zen Cart admin and verify that the configuration menu appears under Modules > Payment.
  6. Copy your Braintree account settings from the Braintree Payments module (installed in step 1).
  7. Log into your Google Pay Console to retrieve your Merchant ID. Note that each subdomain must be verified in the Google Pay Console before the integration will work in Production mode.
  8. Enter any required Google Pay settings (including the verified Merchant ID) in the Braintree Google Pay configuration.
  9. Verify that the Google Pay integration is correctly rendering the payment button on your site.
  10. Test transactions in Sandbox mode before switching to Production.
Tips
  • To complete the Google Pay verification process, we recommend setting the Google Environment to TEST. This will simulate token generation and allow you to capture the necessary screenshots for verification.
    • Screenshot of your product page.
    • Screenshot of your shopping cart page.
    • Screenshot of the Google Pay button rendering correctly on your checkout page.
    • Screenshot of the payment sheet displaying after clicking the Google Pay button.
    • Screenshot of the selected payment method within the Google Pay payment sheet.
    • Screenshot of the order confirmation page after completing a test transaction.
Uninstall
  1. Uninstall the mod first by clicking the Remove button in Modules > Payment > Braintree Google Pay
  2. Remove edits performed in the installation procedure (if applicable).
  3. Delete all uploaded files from your store.
